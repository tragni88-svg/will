## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Display Requirements
- **Screen Size:** 38-inch diagonal (965mm)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 21:9 ultra-wide
- **Resolution:** 7680 × 3240 pixels (8K native)
- **Curvature Radius:** 1800R optimal viewing curve
- **Technology:** Active Matrix OLED (AMOLED)
- **Pixel Density:** 229 PPI
- **Brightness:** 1000 nits peak (HDR10+ certified)
- **Contrast Ratio:** 1,000,000:1 (infinite blacks)
- **Color Gamut:** 100% DCI-P3, 85% Rec.2020
- **Refresh Rate:** 120Hz native (240Hz motion interpolation)
- **Response Time:** <0.1ms (GTG)

### Automotive Integration
- **Operating Temperature:** -40°C to +85°C
- **Storage Temperature:** -50°C to +95°C
- **Humidity Resistance:** 95% RH non-condensing
- **Vibration Rating:** 20G peak (MIL-STD-810H)
- **EMC Compliance:** CISPR 25 Class 5
- **Safety Certification:** AEC-Q100 Grade 2
- **Lifetime:** 15,000 hours @ 50% brightness

### Physical Specifications
- **Overall Dimensions:** 920mm × 390mm × 12mm
- **Bezel Width:** 4mm (ultra-thin border)
- **Weight:** <2.8kg (including protective glass)
- **Mounting Interface:** Custom GM quick-connect system
- **Cable Interface:** Single FPC 60-pin connector
- **Power Consumption:** 45W typical, 85W maximum
