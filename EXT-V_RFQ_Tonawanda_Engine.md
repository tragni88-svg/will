## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Engine Architecture
- **Engine Family:** Gen V Small Block V8 (LT4 variant)
- **Displacement:** 6.2 liters (376 cu in)
- **Configuration:** 90° V8, overhead valve (OHV)
- **Block Material:** Cast aluminum with cast iron liners
- **Head Material:** Cast aluminum (rotocast A356-T6)
- **Compression Ratio:** 10.0:1
- **Bore × Stroke:** 103.25 × 92.0 mm

### Supercharger System
- **Supercharger Type:** Eaton R1740 TVS (Twin Vortices Series)
- **Boost Pressure:** 9.5 psi (0.66 bar) peak
- **Displacement:** 1.7 liters per revolution
- **Drive Ratio:** 2.38:1 (engine to supercharger)
- **Intercooling:** Liquid-to-air (LTA) integrated
- **Intercooler Bricks:** 2 × high-density aluminum

### Performance Targets
- **Peak Power:** 700 hp (522 kW) @ 6,400 rpm
- **Peak Torque:** 650 lb-ft (881 Nm) @ 3,600 rpm
- **Power Density:** 113 hp/liter
- **Specific Output:** 1.67 hp/cu in
- **Redline:** 6,600 rpm (fuel cut-off)
- **Idle Speed:** 600 rpm (±50 rpm)

### Fuel System
- **Fuel Type:** Premium unleaded (91 AKI minimum)
- **Injection:** Direct injection (DI) + port injection (PFI)
- **DI Pressure:** 2,900 psi (200 bar) max
- **Fuel Flow:** 1,250 cc/min per cylinder @ 700hp
- **Octane Requirement:** 91+ AKI (93+ recommended)

### Emissions & Efficiency
- **Emissions Standard:** EPA Tier 3 / CARB LEV III
- **CO2 Emissions:** ≤400 g/mile combined
- **Fuel Economy Target:** 15 mpg city / 21 mpg highway
- **Catalyst System:** Close-coupled + underfloor (4-way)
- **OBD System:** CARB OBD-II compliant
