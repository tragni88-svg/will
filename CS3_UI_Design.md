
# Counter-Strike 3: UI Design

## 1. Main Menu UI

*   **Clean and Minimalist:** The main menu will have a clean, minimalist design, with a focus on ease of use and readability.
*   **Intuitive Navigation:** The navigation will be intuitive and easy to understand, with a clear hierarchy of information.
*   **Featured Content:** The main menu will feature a rotating carousel of featured content, including news, updates, and community highlights.

## 2. In-Game HUD

*   **Modular Design:** The in-game HUD will have a modular design, allowing players to customize the layout to their liking.
*   **Contextual Information:** The HUD will only display information that is relevant to the current situation, reducing clutter and improving readability.
*   **High-Contrast Colors:** The HUD will use high-contrast colors to ensure that it is easily readable in all lighting conditions.

## 3. Art Style

The UI will have a modern, flat design, with a focus on clean lines and simple shapes. The color palette will be a mix of dark grays and blues, with bright, high-contrast colors for important information.
