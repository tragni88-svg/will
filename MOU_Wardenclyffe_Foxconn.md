# Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)

**Between:**

The Wardenclyffe Initiative

**And:**

Foxconn Technology Group

**Effective Date:** January 27, 2026

---

### **Subject: Strategic Partnership for High-Volume Fractal Antenna Etching**

---

This MOU establishes an exclusive partnership for the precision etching of 140,000,000 copper fractal antennas onto customer-supplied metamaterial substrates, as detailed in RFQ-FAE-01.

**Terms of Agreement:**

*   **Unit Price:** Foxconn agrees to a unit price of **$8.50** for the etching process.
*   **Total Contract Value:** The total estimated value of this agreement is **$1,190,000,000 USD**.
*   **Co-Location:** Foxconn agrees to establish a new fabrication facility adjacent to the Wardenclyffe Initiative's Gigafactory Texas to ensure seamless, just-in-time delivery.

This agreement secures a critical step in our manufacturing pipeline. A definitive contract will be executed within 90 days.
