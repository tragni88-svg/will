# Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)

**Between:**

The Wardenclyffe Initiative

**And:**

BASF SE

**Effective Date:** January 27, 2026

---

### **Subject: Strategic Partnership for High-Volume Manufacturing of Polymer Housings**

---

This MOU establishes an exclusive partnership for the injection molding and supply of 140,000,000 polymer housings for the Residential Receiver Module, as detailed in RFQ-HMT-01.

**Terms of Agreement:**

*   **Unit Price:** BASF agrees to a unit price of **$9.50** for the RRM-H1 housing.
*   **Total Contract Value:** The total estimated value of this agreement is **$1,330,000,000 USD**.
*   **Material:** The housing will be fabricated using BASF's specified high-quality, UL94-V0 rated polymer.

This agreement ensures the timely and cost-effective supply of the receiver's protective enclosure. A definitive contract will be executed within 90 days.
