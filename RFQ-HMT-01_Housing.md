# Request for Quotation - RFQ-HMT-01

**To:** DuPont de Nemours, Inc.; BASF SE
**From:** The Wardenclyffe Initiative, Strategic Sourcing Command
**Date:** January 27, 2026
**Subject:** Request for Quotation: High-Volume Injection Molding of Polymer Housings

---

### 1. Introduction

The Wardenclyffe Initiative is requesting quotations for the high-volume injection molding of the exterior housing for our residential power receiver unit.

### 2. Technical Specifications

*   **Designation:** Resonant Receiver Module Housing, Model 1 (RRM-H1)
*   **Material:** High-impact, UV-stabilized polymer with a UL94-V0 flame retardant rating.
*   **Dimensions:** Final exterior dimensions of 450mm x 450mm x 100mm.
*   **Finish:** Standard finish to be a matte, neutral white (color code to be provided). Bids for custom color options are also welcome.
*   **Features:** Must include all specified mounting points, ventilation ports, and access panels for the internal components.

### 3. Production Volume

*   **Total Required Volume:** 140,000,000 units.
*   **Production Ramp-Up:** Aligned with our Gigafactory production schedule, starting with 1,000,000 units in Year 1 and ramping to 25,800,000 units per year.

### 4. Information Requested

1.  **Unit Pricing:** A detailed price-per-unit, including tooling costs.
2.  **Material Specification:** A data sheet for the proposed polymer.
3.  **Manufacturing Capacity:** Confirmation of your ability to meet the production volumes.

**Response Deadline:** Please submit your formal response by March 25, 2026.
