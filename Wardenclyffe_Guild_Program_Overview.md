## 1.0 Welcome to the Wardenclyffe Guild

Welcome, future leaders of the energy revolution. The Wardenclyffe Guild is an exclusive, nationwide network of certified electrical professionals selected to install the Residential Receiver Module (RRM-1) for the Wardenclyffe Initiative.

As a Guild member, you are not just a contractor; you are a trusted partner at the forefront of deploying America's next-generation wireless power grid. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the Guild, its benefits, and the certification process required to join our elite ranks.

## 2.0 The Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every RRM-1 installation is performed to the highest standards of safety, quality, and customer satisfaction. The Guild is the sole authorized body for the installation and activation of Wardenclyffe RRM-1 units.

Joining the Wardenclyffe Guild provides your business with a unique and powerful set of advantages:

*   **Preferred Partner Incentives:** Access to potential benefits such as preferred rates on business insurance and equipment financing.

## 4.0 The 3-Step Certification Process

Certification is designed to be thorough yet efficient, ensuring all members are fully qualified.

### **Step 1: Application & Vetting**

*   **Eligibility:** Applicants must be licensed and insured electrical contracting businesses in good standing.
*   **Process:**
    1.  Submit an online application through the Wardenclyffe Partner Portal.
    2.  Provide proof of business license and current insurance coverage.
    3.  Undergo a standard background check for all client-facing personnel.

### **Step 2: Online Knowledge Certification**

*   **Platform:** The Wardenclyffe Guild Learning Center (online portal).
*   **Curriculum:** A self-paced, 8-module online course covering:
    *   Module 1: The Physics of Wireless Power
    *   Module 2: RRM-1 Technical Deep Dive
    *   Module 3: Site Assessment & Pre-Installation Checklist
    *   Module 4: Standard Installation Procedure
    *   Module 5: System Activation & Commissioning
    *   Module 6: Safety Protocols & Troubleshooting
    *   Module 7: The Wardenclyffe Customer Experience
    *   Module 8: Final Certification Exam
*   **Requirement:** A passing score of 90% or higher on the final exam is required to proceed.

### **Step 3: Hands-On Practical Certification**

*   **Location:** One-day workshops held at regional training centers across the country.
*   **Agenda:**
    *   **Morning Session (4 hours):** RRM-1 physical inspection, advanced troubleshooting, and a guided, hands-on installation on a full-scale mock-up of a residential utility panel.
    *   **Afternoon Session (3 hours):** Practical examination where each participant must successfully complete a timed, end-to-end installation, from site assessment to system activation.
*   **Requirement:** Successful completion of the practical examination, as judged by a Wardenclyffe certified instructor.

## 5.0 Maintaining Guild Status

To maintain certification, Guild members must:

*   Adhere to all installation standards and safety protocols.
*   Maintain a high customer satisfaction rating (CSAT) of 4.5/5.0 stars or higher.
*   Complete a short online recertification module annually to stay current with any technology updates.
