The Tech

The Graviton Displacement Drive (GDD) manipulates local spacetime curvature, creating a directional gravity well. By coupling high-frequency electromagnetic fields with a superconducting mercury-plasma torus, we achieve total mass nullification.

Zero Inertia

Crew experiences 1G regardless of acceleration. Perform 50G maneuvers without physical stress.

Silent Propulsion

No combustion. No exhaust. No sonic booms. Just pure, silent vector thrust.

Quantum Gyroscope

A tri-axial osmium stabilizer anchors the ship in spacetime, allowing for instant vector changes and dead stops.

Infinite Impulse

Powered by the Wardenclyffe Wireless Grid or onboard Fusion. No fuel mass limits your range.
