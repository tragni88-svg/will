# Concept Document: Cadillac EXT-V

## 1. Model Name

**Cadillac EXT-V**

*   **Rationale:** "EXT" pays homage to the original Escalade EXT, re-establishing Cadillac's presence in the truck segment. The "-V" suffix immediately signifies this as a high-performance, top-of-the-line model.

---

## 2. Powertrain

*   **Standard Engine:** 3.0L Duramax Turbo-Diesel I6
    *   **Power:** 305 hp, 495 lb-ft of torque
    *   **Rationale:** Provides excellent low-end torque for towing and hauling, combined with impressive fuel efficiency for daily driving.

*   **V-Series Engine:** 6.2L Supercharged V8 (Hand-built at the Performance Build Center)
    *   **Power:** 700 hp, 650 lb-ft of torque
    *   **Rationale:** This is the heart of the EXT-V. It delivers breathtaking acceleration and a visceral sound, creating a true performance truck that out-muscles key competitors like the Ram TRX.

*   **Transmission:** 10-speed automatic transmission across all trims.
*   **Drivetrain:** Full-time all-wheel drive with a selectable 4-Low mode.

---

## 3. Exterior Design

*   **Front Fascia:** A massive, gloss-black V-series grille with an illuminated Cadillac crest. The signature vertical LED daytime running lights are taller and more aggressive than on the Escalade.
*   **Body:** Crew cab configuration with a 5.5-foot bed. The body lines are sharp and chiseled, with a high beltline and muscular fender flares that accommodate 35-inch all-terrain tires on the V-Series.
*   **Lighting:** Full LED lighting, including animated "welcome" and "farewell" sequences.
*   **Bed:** Carbon fiber composite bed for reduced weight and increased durability. Features a multi-function tailgate (similar to GMC's MultiPro) and an integrated 120V power outlet.
*   **Suspension:** Four-corner Air Ride Adaptive Suspension is standard, allowing for adjustable ride height. The V-Series adds Magnetic Ride Control 4.0 for ultimate on-road performance and off-road compliance.

---

## 4. Interior Design & Technology

*   **Cockpit:** The centerpiece is a 38-inch curved, pillar-to-pillar OLED display, combining the digital instrument cluster and infotainment system into one seamless unit.
*   **Materials:** Semi-aniline leather, open-pore wood, and brushed aluminum are used throughout. The V-Series features carbon fiber accents and exclusive colorways.
*   **Seating:** 16-way power-adjustable front seats with heating, ventilation, and massage functions.
*   **Audio:** AKG Studio Reference 36-speaker audio system, providing an immersive, 3D sound experience.
*   **Super Cruise:** The latest generation of Super Cruise will be standard, allowing for hands-free driving on compatible highways, including a new feature for automated lane changes and trailer-aware assistance.
*   **"V-Mode" Button:** A prominent red button on the steering wheel gives the driver instant access to the EXT-V's full performance potential, sharpening throttle response, suspension, and exhaust note.
