
# Counter-Strike 3: Official Game Modes

## 1. Competitive

*   **5v5 Bomb Defusal:** The classic, high-stakes competitive mode.
*   **CS Pro Matchmaking:** The new, skill-based matchmaking system.
*   **Team Queue:** The new, team-based matchmaking system.

## 2. Casual

*   **10v10 Bomb Defusal:** A more relaxed version of the classic mode, with more players and a more forgiving economy.
*   **Hostage Rescue:** The classic hostage rescue mode, with updated maps and mechanics.

## 3. Deathmatch

*   **Free-for-all:** A fast-paced, chaotic mode where everyone is on their own.
*   **Team Deathmatch:** Two teams battle it out for supremacy.

## 4. Wingman

*   **2v2 Bomb Defusal:** A fast-paced, competitive mode on smaller maps.

## 5. Arms Race

*   **Gun Game:** Players race to get a kill with every weapon in the game.
