# Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)

**Between:**

The Wardenclyffe Initiative, hereafter referred to as "THE INITIATIVE"

**And:**

Schneider Electric SE, hereafter referred to as "THE PARTNER"

**Effective Date:** January 27, 2026

--- 

### **Subject: Strategic Partnership for the High-Volume Manufacture of the Power Conversion Module (PCM-1)**

--- 

### 1. Purpose & Scope

This Memorandum of Understanding establishes an exclusive strategic partnership between THE INITIATIVE and THE PARTNER for the fabrication and supply of the Power Conversion Module (PCM-1). This agreement ensures the supply of the core power electronics for the Wardenclyffe Initiative's residential receiver units and supports the establishment of a domestic supply chain.

### 2. Terms of Agreement

Based on the proposal submitted by THE PARTNER in response to RFQ-RIV-01, both parties agree to the following terms:

*   **Technology Platform:** THE PARTNER will produce the PCM-1 based on their proposed high-efficiency Silicon Carbide (SiC) topology, designed to meet all specifications for power, safety, and reliability.

*   **Production Volume & Pricing:** THE PARTNER agrees to supply a total of **140,000,000 (one hundred forty million)** units of the PCM-1. The unit price is set at **$42.00 USD** for annual volumes of 25,000,000 or more.

*   **Total Contract Value:** The total estimated value of this agreement is **$5,880,000,000 USD**.

*   **Domestic Manufacturing Commitment:** THE PARTNER commits to the construction of **two (2) new manufacturing facilities** within the United States for the exclusive production of the PCM-1. The locations will be chosen to optimize the national distribution strategy.

### 3. Mutual Commitments

*   **THE INITIATIVE** commits to issuing binding purchase orders in accordance with the agreed-upon production schedule and to facilitate site selection for the new factories.

*   **THE PARTNER** commits to the construction, staffing, and operation of the new US-based factories. They will meet the production volumes, quality standards (including a 98.4% efficiency rating), and delivery timelines as specified in their proposal.

### 4. Economic Impact

Both parties acknowledge the significant positive economic impact of this agreement, including the creation of an estimated 4,000 American manufacturing jobs and the strengthening of the domestic industrial base.

--- 

This MOU represents a foundational agreement for the industrial-scale rollout of the Wardenclyffe Initiative. A definitive, legally binding contract will be drafted and executed within 90 days.
