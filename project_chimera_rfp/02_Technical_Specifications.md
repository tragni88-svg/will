# Project Chimera: Technical Specifications

## 1.0 Airframe and Structure
- **Configuration:** Lenticular (saucer) profile
- **Diameter:** 24 meters
- **Height:** 6 meters
- **Primary Material:** Carbon-fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP) composite for primary structure.
- **Substructure:** Titanium alloy for high-stress load-bearing elements, particularly around the propulsion and reactor housing.
- **Landing Gear:** Retractable tricycle landing gear with oleo-pneumatic struts.

## 2.0 Propulsion System: MHD Toroidal Drive
- **Type:** Magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) accelerator, open-cycle, using ambient air as propellant.
- **Geometry:** 8-segment toroidal channel.
- **Magnetic Field Generation:** 8 superconducting YBCO (Yttrium Barium Copper Oxide) magnets.
- **Field Strength:** 5.0 Tesla (nominal).
- **Plasma Generation:** Microwave and RF ionization arrays at the inlet of each segment.
- **Thrust Control:** Achieved by modulating plasma velocity and density via the PD flight control system.

## 3.0 Power Generation and Distribution
- **Power Source:** Compact, lead-cooled fast reactor (LFR).
- **Thermal Output:** 50 MWth (megawatts thermal).
- **Electrical Output:** 20 MWe (megawatts electrical) via a closed-cycle Brayton turbine.
- **Shielding:** Borated polyethylene and depleted uranium layers for neutron and gamma radiation shielding.
- **Power Distribution:** Superconducting busbars distributing high-voltage DC power to MHD segments and avionics.

## 4.0 Avionics and Flight Control
- **Flight Computer:** Triple-redundant flight control system.
- **Control Law:** Proportional-Derivative (PD) control loop for attitude stabilization and trajectory tracking.
- **Navigation:** Redundant INS/GPS coupled with a terrain-following radar system.
- **Sensors:** Distributed pressure and temperature sensors across the airframe and within MHD channels for real-time feedback to the flight controller.
