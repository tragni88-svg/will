## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Composite Bed Requirements
- **Overall Length:** 1,930mm (76 inches)
- **Overall Width:** 1,650mm (65 inches)  
- **Bed Depth:** 560mm (22 inches)
- **Material System:** Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer (CFRP)
- **Fiber Type:** Toray T800S intermediate modulus
- **Resin System:** Toughened epoxy (Benzoxazine preferred)
- **Fiber Volume Fraction:** 58-62%
- **Layup Architecture:** Quasi-isotropic [0/45/90/-45]s

### Mechanical Properties
- **Tensile Strength:** ≥2,800 MPa (longitudinal)
- **Tensile Modulus:** ≥165 GPa (longitudinal)
- **Compressive Strength:** ≥1,500 MPa
- **Flexural Strength:** ≥2,200 MPa
- **Interlaminar Shear:** ≥120 MPa
- **Impact Resistance:** ≥50 J/mm (CAI after 6.7J impact)

### Weight Targets
- **Bed Assembly Target:** ≤45 kg (99 lbs)
- **Weight Savings vs Steel:** ≥40%
- **Weight Savings vs Aluminum:** ≥20%
- **Areal Weight:** 450 g/m² (prepreg basis)

### Manufacturing Requirements
- **Process Method:** Resin Transfer Molding (RTM) preferred
- **Cure Cycle:** ≤180°C for 2 hours maximum
- **Surface Finish:** Class A on exterior surfaces
- **Dimensional Tolerance:** ±0.5mm on critical interfaces
- **Wall Thickness:** 2.5mm nominal (variable by zone)

## INTEGRATION FEATURES

### Mounting & Attachment Points
- **Frame Interface:** 12 high-strength steel inserts
- **Tailgate Hinges:** Integrated carbon fiber pockets

- **Cargo Management:** T-slot rail system integration
