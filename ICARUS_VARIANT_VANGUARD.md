# Icarus-V "Vanguard" Military Spaceplane Specification

## 1. Overview
The **Icarus-V (Vanguard)** is the militarized variant of the Project Icarus platform. Manufactured by **Boeing Defense, Space & Security** under license from Wardenclyffe USA, it is designed for space superiority, orbital bombardment, and rapid global strike missions.

## 2. Technical Specifications

*   **Manufacturer:** Boeing / Wardenclyffe
*   **Role:** Space Superiority Fighter / Interceptor
*   **Crew:** 1 (Pilot) + AI Co-Pilot (Polaris-M)
*   **Dimensions:**
    *   Length: 18 meters
    *   Wingspan: 12 meters (Variable Geometry for atmospheric combat)
*   **Propulsion:**
    *   **Core:** Wardenclyffe GDC-Mk3 (High-Output)
    *   **Power:** Compact Fusion Reactor (5.0 GW)
    *   **Performance:** 50G+ Maneuverability, Mach 30+ Atmospheric, 0.2c Interplanetary.

## 3. Armament (Helios Integration)

The Vanguard is built around the **Helios Weapon System**:

*   **Primary:** 2x **H-V2 "Glaive" Directed Energy Cannons** (Chin-mounted).
    *   *Effect:* Kinetic/Thermal hybrid beams capable of vaporizing aircraft and disabling satellite shielding.
*   **Secondary:** Internal Weapons Bay (Capacity: 4,000 kg).
    *   *Loadout:* Hypersonic missiles, Kinetic Rods from God (orbital strike), or additional sensor pallets.
*   **Defense:**
    *   **Gravitic Deflector:** Vectors incoming fire around the hull.
    *   **Quantum Stealth:** Bends light around the hull, rendering the craft invisible to visual and sensor detection.

## 4. Avionics & Systems

*   **Radar:** Quantum Radar (lidar/gravimetric hybrid) - Cannot be jammed.
*   **AI:** **Polaris-M (Military)** - Handles G-force compensation, target tracking, and electronic warfare.
*   **Stabilization:** Dual-Redundant Quantum Gyroscopes for "Dead Stop" combat maneuvers.

## 5. Production Status
*   **Prime Contractor:** Boeing (St. Louis Phantom Works)
*   **Initial Order:** 50 Units
*   **Delivery:** Q3 2026
