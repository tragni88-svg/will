## 3. Presale Lineup & Game Details

This section details the three launch titles that will be featured on the website and available for presale.

### Game 1: Counter-Strike 3

*   **Genre:** Competitive Tactical Shooter
*   **Logline:** The legend returns. 10K resolution, 100 FPS, 1.6-style fixed recoil, and per-polygon hitboxes. The ultimate competitive experience.
*   **Presale Editions:**
    *   **Standard Edition ($69.99):** Base Game.
    *   **Global Elite Edition ($89.99):** Base Game, "Classic Collection" Weapon Skin Pack (AK-47, M4A4, AWP, Deagle), Digital Soundtrack & Artbook.
    *   **Esports Champion's Edition ($179.99):** All digital content, plus a 1:1 scale replica of the in-game Karambit knife (Sapphire), a steelbook case, and a hardcover artbook signed by the developers.

### Game 2: Aethelgard: The Crimson Covenant

*   **Genre:** Open-World Fantasy RPG
*   **Logline:** In a realm fractured by a forgotten war between gods and mortals, a cursed hero must forge a new destiny. Your choices will either save Aethelgard or drown it in shadow.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **Dynamic World:** A massive, handcrafted world that reacts to your decisions. Entire cities can thrive or fall based on your actions.
    *   **Classless Progression:** Build your character freely, mixing and matching abilities from dozens of skill trees.
    *   **Next-Gen Graphics:** Built in Unreal Engine 5, leveraging Nanite for unparalleled environmental detail.
*   **Presale Editions:**
    *   **Standard Edition ($69.99):** Base Game.
    *   **Covenant Edition ($89.99):** Base Game, "Shadow-Sworn" Armor Set, a unique "Bloodhound" mount.
    *   **Aethelgard Collector's Edition ($179.99):** All digital content, a high-quality resin statue of the protagonist, a cloth map of the world, and a collector's box.

### Game 3: Supernova Circuit

*   **Genre:** Anti-Gravity Sci-Fi Racer
*   **Logline:** In the neon-drenched cityscapes of the 23rd century, pilot bleeding-edge anti-gravity racers in a high-stakes, high-speed tournament where only the fastest survive.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **Deep Customization:** Build your racer from the ground up, tuning everything from engine output to shield modulation.
    *   **Dynamic Tracks:** Race through sprawling mega-cities, asteroid fields, and alien jungles. Tracks can change mid-race due to environmental events.
    *   **VR-First Design:** Engineered for a breathtaking sense of speed and immersion in VR.
*   **Presale Editions:**
    *   **Standard Edition ($59.99):** Base Game.
    *   **Grid-Runner Edition ($79.99):** Base Game, exclusive "Chrome-Burn" vehicle chassis, and a pack of unique decals.
    *   **Supernova Collector's Edition ($159.99):** All digital content, a 1:24 scale model of the "Vortex" flagship racer, and a themed steelbook case.
