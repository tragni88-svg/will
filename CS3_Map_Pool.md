
# Counter-Strike 3: Official Map Pool

## 1. Active Duty Map Pool

The following maps will be available in the Active Duty map pool for competitive matchmaking at launch:

*   **de_foundry:** The new, flagship map for *Counter-Strike 3*.
*   **de_dust2:** A timeless classic, re-imagined with 10K textures and new lighting.
*   **de_mirage:** A fan-favorite, updated with improved visibility and minor balance tweaks.
*   **de_inferno:** A strategic masterpiece, enhanced with new environmental details.
*   **de_nuke:** The vertical battleground, re-balanced for more competitive play.
*   **de_overpass:** The fast-paced, dynamic map, with new routes and flanking opportunities.
*   **de_vertigo:** The high-stakes, high-altitude thriller, with a redesigned B-site.

## 2. Reserve Map Pool

The following maps will be available in the Reserve map pool for casual and scrimmage modes:

*   **de_train**
*   **de_cache**
*   **de_cbble**
*   **cs_office**
