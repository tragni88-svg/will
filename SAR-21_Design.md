
# SAR-21: Weapon Design Document

## 1. Overview

The **SAR-21** is a new, assault rifle for *Counter-Strike 3*. It is a versatile weapon that is designed to bridge the gap between the M4A4 and the AK-47. It is a Terrorist-exclusive weapon, available for $2900.

## 2. Stats

*   **Damage:** 33
*   **Rate of Fire:** 600 RPM
*   **Recoil:** Moderate
*   **Accuracy:** High
*   **Magazine Size:** 25

## 3. Recoil Pattern

The SAR-21 will have a unique, S-shaped recoil pattern that is challenging to master but highly rewarding. The first 5-6 shots will be relatively accurate, but the recoil will become more difficult to control as the spray continues.

## 4. Role in the Meta

The SAR-21 is designed to be a versatile weapon that can be used in a variety of situations. It is effective at both long and short range, and it is a good choice for players who want a weapon that can do it all. It is a direct competitor to the M4A4, and it will be interesting to see how the two weapons stack up against each other in the new meta.
