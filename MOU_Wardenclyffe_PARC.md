# Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)

**Between:**

The Wardenclyffe Initiative, hereafter referred to as "THE INITIATIVE"

**And:**

Palo Alto Research Center (PARC), hereafter referred to as "THE SUPPLIER"

**Effective Date:** January 27, 2026

--- 

### **Subject: Strategic Partnership for the Mass Production of Metamaterial Substrates**

--- 

### 1. Purpose & Scope

This Memorandum of Understanding establishes a strategic partnership between THE INITIATIVE and THE SUPPLIER for the exclusive fabrication of the **Resonant Receiver Substrate, Mark I**, as detailed in RFQ-MTS-01. This agreement is the cornerstone of the manufacturing and deployment phase of the Wardenclyffe Initiative.

### 2. Terms of Agreement

Both parties hereby agree to the terms outlined in THE SUPPLIER's formal response dated February 28, 2026:

*   **Total Production Volume:** THE SUPPLIER agrees to produce and deliver a total of **140,000,000 (one hundred forty million)** units of the specified substrate.

*   **Pricing:** The price per unit is set at **$175.00 USD** for annual volumes of 25,000,000 or more, bringing the total estimated contract value to **$25,550,000,000 USD**.

*   **Joint Capital Investment:** Both parties agree to the co-funding and construction of a dedicated **"Metamaterial Foundry."** THE INITIATIVE will provide 75% of the required capital for the foundry's construction, estimated at $1.2 Billion USD. The foundry will be located at a mutually agreed-upon site adjacent to one of THE INITIATIVE's planned Gigafactories.

*   **Timeline:** The project timeline will commence upon the execution of this MOU. THE SUPPLIER commits to a **22-month timeline** for the construction of the foundry and the delivery of the first 1,000,000 substrate units.

### 3. Mutual Commitments

*   **THE INITIATIVE** commits to providing the necessary capital investment on schedule and to issue binding purchase orders in accordance with the agreed-upon production ramp-up.

*   **THE SUPPLIER** commits to meeting the production volumes, quality standards, and delivery timelines as specified. They will provide THE INITIATIVE with transparent progress reports on a quarterly basis.

### 4. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding the proprietary technologies and financial terms involved in this agreement.

--- 

This MOU represents a firm commitment from both parties to proceed with this historic undertaking. A definitive, legally binding contract will be drafted and executed within 90 days of this memorandum.
