## Step 2: Prepare Required Documents

To open a business bank account, you will typically need the following. Having these ready will speed up the application process.

*   **For Sole Proprietors:**
    *   Social Security Number (SSN)
    *   Government-issued photo ID (e.g., Driver's License, Passport)
    *   Proof of address

*   **For LLCs or Corporations:**
    *   Employer Identification Number (EIN) from the IRS
    *   Articles of Organization (for LLCs) or Articles of Incorporation (for Corporations)
    *   Business Address and Phone Number
    *   Government-issued photo ID for all owners with 25% or more ownership

---

## Step 3: Open the Account

1.  **Select a Bank:** Choose one of the recommended banks from Step 1.
2.  **Visit the Website:** Use the provided link to go to the bank's website.
3.  **Start the Application:** Look for "Open an Account" or "Get Started."
4.  **Submit Information:** Fill out the online application with your personal and business details.
5.  **Upload Documents:** Upload the required documents from Step 2.
6.  **Await Approval:** The bank will review your application. Approval can be instant or take a few business days.

---

## Step 4: Link Your New Bank Account to Seller Platforms

Once your bank account is approved, you will receive an **Account Number** and a **Routing Number**. Use these to configure your payout settings on each platform.

### Shopify Payouts:

1.  Log in to your Shopify Admin.
2.  Go to **Settings** > **Payments**.
3.  In the **Shopify Payments** section, click **Manage**.
4.  Under **Payout account**, click **Change bank account**.
5.  Enter your new account and routing number.
6.  **Crucially, ensure the name on the bank account is an exact match for "William Tragni"** to avoid payout failures.

### Amazon Seller Central Payouts:

1.  Log in to Amazon Seller Central.
2.  Go to **Settings** > **Account Info**.
3.  Click on **Bank Account Information**.
4.  Click **Add new deposit method**.
5.  Select your marketplace (e.g., Amazon.com).
6.  Enter your new account and routing number.
7.  **The "Account Holder Name" must be "William Tragni".**

### eBay Managed Payments:

1.  Log in to your eBay Seller Hub.
2.  Go to the **Payments** tab.
3.  In the **Payouts** section, find your linked bank account and select **Edit**.
4.  You will be prompted to add a new bank account.
5.  Enter your new account and routing number.
6.  eBay will send two micro-deposits to verify the account. Check your new bank account in 1-3 days for these deposits and enter the amounts in eBay to confirm.
7.  **The name on the bank account must match your eBay account name ("William Tragni").**
