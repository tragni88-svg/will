
# de_foundry: Map Design Document

## 1. Overview

**de_foundry** is a new, competitive bomb defusal map for *Counter-Strike 3*. Set in a decommissioned industrial foundry, the map is designed to be a fast-paced, tactical battleground with a focus on verticality and tight chokepoints.

## 2. Layout and Callouts

*   **T-Spawn:** A large, open area with multiple exits.
*   **A-Site:** A large, open area with a central, elevated platform. The bombsite is located on the platform.
    *   **Callouts:** Catwalk, Silo, Crane, Platform
*   **B-Site:** A smaller, more enclosed site with multiple levels. The bombsite is located on the lower level.
    *   **Callouts:** Tunnels, Vents, Lower, Upper
*   **Mid:** A long, narrow corridor that connects the two bombsites.
    *   **Callouts:** Mid, Top Mid, Bottom Mid

## 3. Key Features

*   **Verticality:** The map features a significant amount of verticality, with multiple levels and elevated positions. This will create new opportunities for skill-based jumps and creative strategies.
*   **Destructible Environments:** Certain walls and floors will be destructible, allowing players to create new lines of sight and flanking routes.
*   **Dynamic Lighting:** The map will feature dynamic lighting that changes throughout the match, creating new challenges and opportunities.

## 4. Art Style

The map will have a gritty, industrial art style, with a focus on realism and detail. The color palette will be muted, with a focus on grays, browns, and rust tones. This will create a visually distinct and immersive environment that is also highly readable.
